### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
* **How do I sync my sessions?** Sessions sync automatically to your iPhone once you finish and save the workout on your Apple Watch.
* **Can I use the Digital Crown for the Timer?** Yes! Use the Digital Crown to adjust the countdown time precisely, even with wet hands or gloves.
* **Is the GPS tracking waterproof-safe?** The app uses `HKWorkoutSession` to keep tracking active even if the screen locks or is touched by water.

## Privacy Policy
Your privacy is paramount. RACE TIMER does not sell or share your personal data.

### 1. Data Collection
* **Location:** We use GPS to calculate speed, distance to buoys, and detect maneuvers. This data is stored locally on your devices and synced via your private iCloud/WatchConnectivity.
* **Health Data:** We access Heart Rate and Calories through HealthKit to provide sports analytics. This data never leaves the Apple Health ecosystem.
* **Motion Sensors:** Gyroscope data is used strictly for maneuver detection and fall identification.

### 2. Data Usage
All data is used exclusively to provide you with performance analytics of your sailing sessions. No data is sent to external servers.

### 3. User Control
You can revoke location or health permissions at any time through the iOS/watchOS Settings app.
