# Privacy Policy — RacerTime

**Last updated:** April 29, 2026

## 1. Introduction

RacerTime ("the app", "we") is a race timing and session analysis application for water sports, developed by WindPowered. This privacy policy describes what data the app collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

---

## 2. Data the app collects

### 2.1 Location data (GPS)

- **What:** GPS coordinates during active recording sessions.
- **Why:** To record the route and calculate speed, distance, and maneuver analysis.
- **Where it is stored:** Exclusively on the user's device. It is not transmitted to external servers.
- **When it is accessed:** Only while a session is active. The "While Using the App" permission is required.

### 2.2 Health data (HealthKit)

- **What:** Heart rate during sessions.
- **Why:** To display real-time heart rate on the Apple Watch and save it alongside the session.
- **Where it is stored:** In Apple Health on the device. RacerTime does not send health data outside the device.
- **Permissions:** Read and write access to HealthKit, explicitly requested from the user.

### 2.3 Motion and gyroscope data

- **What:** Accelerometer and gyroscope data from the Apple Watch.
- **Why:** Automatic detection of maneuvers (tacks, gybes) during a session.
- **Where it is stored:** Processed in real time on the device. No raw motion data is stored.

### 2.4 Photos

- **What:** Screenshots of session summaries.
- **Why:** To save images to the user's photo library upon explicit request.
- **Permissions:** Write-only access to the photo library. The app does not read existing photos.

### 2.5 User name (Sign in with Apple)

- **What:** Name provided by Apple upon first sign-in.
- **Why:** Profile personalization within the app.
- **Where it is stored:** Locally in the device's Keychain. It is not sent to external servers.
- **Note:** Apple only provides the name on the first sign-in. The user can edit it manually from within the app.

### 2.6 Purchase data (StoreKit / App Store)

- **What:** Active subscription status (RacerTime Pro).
- **Why:** To verify access to premium features.
- **How:** Managed entirely by the Apple App Store. RacerTime does not store or process payment data.
- **Sync:** Subscription status is synchronized between iPhone and Apple Watch locally via WatchConnectivity, with a maximum validity of 24 hours without confirmation.

---

## 3. Data the app does NOT collect

- ❌ Does not collect usage data or behavioral analytics.
- ❌ Does not use third-party tools such as Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or similar.
- ❌ Does not display advertising or share data with ad networks.
- ❌ Does not transmit session, GPS, health, or motion data to any external server.
- ❌ Does not create user profiles on external servers.

---

## 4. Storage and security

All data generated by the app (sessions, GPS routes, heart rate, settings) is stored **locally on the user's device** using:

- `UserDefaults` for settings and preferences.
- `Keychain` for sensitive identity data (username, session tokens).
- Local files in the app sandbox for session history.

There is no RacerTime backend server that stores personal data.

---

## 5. Third-party data

### Apple App Store / StoreKit

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple. Please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) for more information on how Apple handles payment data.

### HealthKit

Health data is stored in Apple Health and is subject to Apple's privacy policy.

---

## 6. Children's privacy

RacerTime is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

---

## 7. Your rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- You can **delete all data** by uninstalling the app.
- You can **revoke permissions** (location, health, motion, photos) at any time from **Settings → Privacy** on your iPhone or Apple Watch.
- Health data in HealthKit can be managed from Apple's **Health** app.
